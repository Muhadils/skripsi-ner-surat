SLIDE 9 - KONFIGURASI TRAINING
==================================================
Judul Slide:
Konfigurasi Training

Isi Slide:
- Base model: IndoBERT (indobenchmark/indobert-lite-base-p2)
- Optimizer: AdamW
- Learning rate: 5e-5
- Batch size: 8
- Epoch: 3
- Max sequence length: 512
- Loss: CrossEntropyLoss dengan class weights

Saran Gambar:
- Jenis gambar: Tabel parameter training.
- Deskripsi gambar: Tabel 2 kolom (Parameter dan Nilai) agar mudah dibaca dosen penguji.
